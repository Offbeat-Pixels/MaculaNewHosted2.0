Sales as a Service

Sales as a Service– At Macula we assist you to expand your product to different markets and geographies by being your virtual sales support. We understand that on one hand where Marketing is relatively easy to come by, it is the sales process that invariably becomes a bottle-neck in successful closure of clients, over that the increased competition in the market mandates a pointed sales effort that is well targeted and executed by a well experienced sales team. The team at Macula Consultants have several decades of collective sales experience that cuts across products, sectors & geographies, competent enough to front-end your sales process.

GTM Strategy – We assist you to map your customer profile based on geography, sector, & persona, and also develop the right positioning for your product by evaluating the competitors and their pricing structure. All in all we plan your GTM to have an effective launch of your product by creating the right value proposition which would have a shorter sales cycle directly impacting your top line.

Sales Consulting– We use deep industry experience to audit your existing sales cycle to identify the gaps and create effective sales processes by reviewing and improving the existing sales strategies, sales closure cycles, sales talking points, value proposition, lead generation process to help you to operate at your full potential.
